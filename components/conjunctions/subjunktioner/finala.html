What is a Final Subjunction?

Final subjunctions (finala subjunktioner) introduce subordinate clauses that express purpose, intention, goal, or desired result.

They answer questions such as why?, for what purpose?, or in order to achieve what?.

Important

  • Final subjunctions express purpose rather than cause.
  • They explain what someone intends to achieve.
  • Many final constructions contain an infinitive.
  • Some final subjunctions can also express means or method.
  • Purpose clauses often correspond to English to, in order to, or so that.
Subjunktion Function Example Translations
för att För att is the most common Swedish expression of purpose.

It is followed by an infinitive and corresponds to English to or in order to.

It explains the purpose or intention behind an action.
Purpose
Jag studerar för att klara provet. 		Purpose
EN: I study to pass the exam.
DE: Ich lerne, um die Prüfung zu bestehen.
FR: J'étudie pour réussir l'examen.
IT: Studio per superare l'esame.
ES: Estudio para aprobar el examen.
så att Så att introduces a clause expressing intended result or consequence.

It corresponds to English so that.

Unlike för att, it is followed by a complete clause.
Purpose
Hon sprang snabbt så att hon hann med bussen. 		Purpose
EN: She ran quickly so that she could catch the bus.
DE: Sie lief schnell, damit sie den Bus erreichte.
FR: Elle a couru vite afin qu'elle attrape le bus.
IT: Corse velocemente così che riuscisse a prendere l'autobus.
ES: Corrió rápido para que pudiera alcanzar el autobús.
genom att Genom att expresses the means, method, or process by which something is achieved.

It corresponds to English by + gerund.

The construction is followed by an infinitive.
Means
Han förbättrade sitt uttal genom att lyssna på radio. 		Means
EN: He improved his pronunciation by listening to the radio.
DE: Er verbesserte seine Aussprache indem er Radio hörte.
FR: Il a amélioré sa prononciation en écoutant la radio.

ES: Mejoró su pronunciación escuchando la radio.
