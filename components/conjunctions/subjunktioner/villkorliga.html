What is a Conditional Subjunction?

Conditional subjunctions (villkorliga subjunktioner) introduce subordinate clauses that express a condition, requirement, exception, or hypothetical situation.

They answer questions such as under what condition?, provided that?, or unless?.

Important

  • Conditional subjunctions introduce subordinate clauses.
  • They express a condition that must be fulfilled for something else to happen.
  • Subordinate clauses follow subordinate-clause word order.
  • Sentence adverbials such as inte normally appear before the finite verb.
  • Some conditional subjunctions are common in everyday speech, while others belong primarily to formal written language.
Subjunktion Function Example Translations
om Om is the most common conditional subjunction in Swedish.

It introduces a condition, possibility, or hypothetical situation.

It corresponds most closely to English if.
Condition
Om du är snäll, ska du få godis. 		Condition
EN: If you are good, you will get candy.
DE: Wenn du brav bist, bekommst du Süßigkeiten.
FR: Si tu es sage, tu auras des bonbons.
IT: Se fai il bravo, avrai delle caramelle.
ES: Si te portas bien, recibirás dulces.
Hypothetical Situation
Om det regnar, stannar vi hemma. 		Hypothetical Situation
EN: If it rains, we will stay at home.
DE: Wenn es regnet, bleiben wir zu Hause.
FR: S'il pleut, nous resterons à la maison.
IT: Se piove, resteremo a casa.
ES: Si llueve, nos quedaremos en casa.
ifall Ifall is very similar to om.

It often sounds slightly more cautious, tentative, or conversational.

In many contexts, ifall and om can be used interchangeably.
Indirect Question
De undrade ifall vi ville följa med. 		Indirect Question
EN: They wondered whether we wanted to come along.
DE: Sie fragten sich, ob wir mitkommen wollten.
FR: Ils se demandaient si nous voulions les accompagner.
IT: Si chiedevano se volessimo venire con loro.
ES: Se preguntaban si queríamos acompañarlos.
Precaution
Ring mig ifall du inte hittar. 		Precaution
EN: Call me if you cannot find your way.
DE: Ruf mich an, falls du den Weg nicht findest.
FR: Appelle-moi si tu ne trouves pas ton chemin.
IT: Chiamami se non riesci a trovare la strada.
ES: Llámame si no encuentras el camino.
såvida Såvida expresses an exception or negative condition.

It is often used together with inte and corresponds roughly to English unless.

It is more common in formal written language than in everyday speech.
Exception
Jag följer med såvida ni inte har någonting emot det. 		Exception
EN: I will come along unless you object.
DE: Ich komme mit, sofern ihr nichts dagegen habt.
FR: Je vous accompagne à moins que cela ne vous dérange.
IT: Verrò con voi a meno che non abbiate obiezioni.
ES: Iré con vosotros a menos que tengáis algo en contra.
Condition
Allt kommer att gå bra såvida inga problem uppstår. 		Condition
EN: Everything will be fine provided that no problems arise.
DE: Alles wird gut gehen, sofern keine Probleme auftreten.
FR: Tout ira bien à condition que aucun problème ne survienne.
IT: Tutto andrà bene purché non sorgano problemi.
ES: Todo irá bien siempre que no surjan problemas.
huruvida Huruvida introduces indirect yes–no questions.

It corresponds roughly to English whether.

It is common in formal and academic language.
Indirect Question
Det är fråga om huruvida han kommer att lyckas. 		Indirect Question
EN: The question is whether he will succeed.
DE: Die Frage ist, ob er Erfolg haben wird.
FR: La question est de savoir si il réussira.
IT: La questione è se riuscirà.
ES: La cuestión es si tendrá éxito.
förutsatt att Förutsatt att is a formal expression used to introduce a strict condition.

It corresponds to English provided that or on condition that.
Strict Condition
Förutsatt att informationen är korrekt är huset nu till salu. 		Strict Condition
EN: Provided that the information is correct, the house is now for sale.
DE: Vorausgesetzt, dass die Information korrekt ist, steht das Haus nun zum Verkauf.
FR: À condition que l'information soit correcte, la maison est désormais en vente.
IT: A condizione che l'informazione sia corretta, la casa è ora in vendita.
ES: Siempre que la información sea correcta, la casa está ahora en venta.
så länge som Så länge som expresses a condition that must remain true.

It corresponds closely to English as long as.

The expression is common in both spoken and written Swedish.
Continuing Condition
Så länge som du följer reglerna får du vara kvar. 		Continuing Condition
EN: As long as you follow the rules, you may stay.
DE: Solange du die Regeln befolgst, darfst du bleiben.
FR: Tant que tu respectes les règles, tu peux rester.
IT: Finché rispetti le regole, puoi restare.
ES: Mientras sigas las reglas, puedes quedarte.
Continuing Condition
Allt kommer att gå bra så länge som vi arbetar tillsammans. 		Continuing Condition
EN: Everything will be fine as long as we work together.
DE: Alles wird gut gehen, solange wir zusammenarbeiten.
FR: Tout ira bien tant que nous travaillerons ensemble.
IT: Tutto andrà bene finché lavoreremo insieme.
ES: Todo irá bien mientras trabajemos juntos.
bara om Bara om introduces a restrictive condition.

It corresponds to English only if.

Unlike ordinary om, it emphasizes that no other condition is sufficient.
Restriction
Du får låna bilen bara om du tankar den efteråt. 		Restriction
EN: You may borrow the car only if you refuel it afterwards.
DE: Du darfst das Auto benutzen nur wenn du es anschließend auftankst.
FR: Tu peux emprunter la voiture seulement si tu fais le plein ensuite.
IT: Puoi prendere in prestito l'auto solo se fai il pieno dopo.
ES: Puedes tomar prestado el coche solo si lo llenas de combustible después.
Restriction
Biljetten gäller bara om du kan visa legitimation. 		Restriction
EN: The ticket is valid only if you can show identification.
DE: Das Ticket gilt nur wenn du einen Ausweis vorzeigen kannst.
FR: Le billet est valable seulement si tu peux présenter une pièce d'identité.
IT: Il biglietto è valido solo se puoi mostrare un documento d'identità.
ES: El billete es válido solo si puedes mostrar una identificación.
