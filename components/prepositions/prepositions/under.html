Under

The Swedish preposition under most commonly means under, below, or beneath and is used to describe position or movement below something else. It can also express duration, meaning during, and is frequently used in expressions involving measurements, age, limits, and control.

Use Meaning Example Translations
Location
Location Indicates a position below something else. Boken ligger under bordet. EN: The book is under the table.
DE: Das Buch liegt unter dem Tisch.
FR: Le livre est sous la table.
IT: Il libro è sotto il tavolo.
ES: El libro está debajo de la mesa.
Location Indicates movement beneath something. Bilen körde under bron. EN: The car drove under the bridge.
DE: Das Auto fuhr unter der Brücke hindurch.
FR: La voiture est passée sous le pont.
IT: L'auto è passata sotto il ponte.
ES: El coche pasó debajo de el puente.
State Used in fixed expressions describing control or supervision. Är allt under kontroll? EN: Is everything under control?
DE: Ist alles unter Kontrolle?
FR: Tout est-il sous contrôle ?
IT: È tutto sotto controllo?
ES: ¿Está todo bajo control?
Measure / Limit
Measure Indicates an age below a specified limit. Barn under femton år. EN: Children under fifteen years old.
DE: Kinder unter fünfzehn Jahren.
FR: Les enfants de moins de quinze ans.
IT: Bambini sotto i quindici anni.
ES: Niños menores de quince años.
Measure Indicates a value below a certain level. Vid temperaturer under noll. EN: At temperatures below zero.
DE: Bei Temperaturen unter null Grad.
FR: À des températures inférieures à zéro.
IT: A temperature sotto lo zero.
ES: A temperaturas por debajo de cero.
Time / Duration
Duration Indicates that something happened during a period of time. Vad gjorde du under kriget? EN: What did you do during the war?
DE: Was hast du während des Krieges gemacht?
FR: Qu'as-tu fait pendant la guerre ?
IT: Che cosa hai fatto durante la guerra?
ES: ¿Qué hiciste durante la guerra?
Duration Indicates that something developed during an ongoing event. Vi blev goda vänner under samtalets gång. EN: We became close friends during the course of the conversation.
DE: Wir wurden während des Gesprächs gute Freunde.
FR: Nous sommes devenus de bons amis au cours de la conversation.
IT: Siamo diventati buoni amici durante la conversazione.
ES: Nos hicimos buenos amigos durante la conversación.
