About this project

About

Grammar and Vocabulary is a free educational resource designed to help learners practise Swedish grammar and vocabulary through theory, examples, and interactive exercises.

About the Website

This website was created to provide clear and structured explanations of Swedish grammar. The material focuses on practical language use and combines concise theory with exercises that allow learners to test their understanding immediately.

New topics, exercises, and reference materials are added regularly.

About the Author

My name is Mike, and I work as a language teacher, translator, and educator. Over the years I have taught students from a wide range of linguistic and cultural backgrounds.

This project was created to make Swedish grammar more accessible through clear explanations, multilingual comparisons, and self-correcting exercises.

Feedback and Corrections

Although every effort is made to ensure accuracy, mistakes may occasionally occur.

If you notice a typo, grammatical error, broken exercise, incorrect translation, or unclear explanation, please feel free to get in touch.

Constructive feedback helps improve the website for all learners.
